About the Unit

Task Force Meridian is a Halo-themed MILSIM unit focused on immersive infantry operations, tactical coordination, and cooperative gameplay. Operating as a platoon-sized force, we rotate through thematic campaigns and prioritize teamwork, discipline, and adaptability in the field.

Our members come from all backgrounds, united by our love of sci-fi realism and professional military simulation. Whether clearing Covenant strongholds or defending human colonies, we fight with precision and pride.
